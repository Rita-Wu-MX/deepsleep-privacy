Privacy Policy

DeepSleep · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

DeepSleep is a sleep sounds and guided meditation app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. DeepSleep does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All audio content runs entirely on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

Data Type Collected?
Name, email, or account info✗ Never
Location data✗ Never
Health or fitness data✗ Never
Device identifiers or advertising IDs✗ Never
Usage analytics or crash reports✗ Never
Microphone or camera access✗ Never

Data Deletion & Retention

Since DeepSleep does not collect or store any personal data on its servers, there is no user data to delete. Any app-related settings remain strictly on your local device and are removed when the app is uninstalled.

In-App Purchases

DeepSleep offers optional, one-time tip purchases (Tip Jar) processed entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

  • All transactions are handled by Apple's StoreKit framework.
  • Tips are consumable purchases — no content is locked or unlocked.
  • No restore functionality is required or provided.

Audio & Permissions

All audio files (nature sounds, zen tones, and guided meditation tracks) are bundled within the app. No streaming, no internet connection required for playback. DeepSleep requests only the following system permission:

  • Audio Playback Background audio via AVAudioSession — required to continue playing when the screen is locked.

Third-Party Services

DeepSleep does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social login providers. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, governed by Apple.

Children's Privacy

DeepSleep is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, there are no special risks for children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
